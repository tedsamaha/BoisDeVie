Company Profile


Bois De Vie, based in Mansourieh Lebanon, in 2014. Our primary business is to supply architects, interior designers and residential clients with all forms and styles of hand-made furniture, cabinetry or architectural woodwork. Since its inception, BDV has supplied museum quality custom furniture for residential and select corporate environments garnering the respect of leading architects and designers worldwide

Bois De Vie actively takes part in furniture making while also directing the work of five senior cabinetmakers. Most of our employees have been with the company since the start. Each project is managed on a daily basis by meetings between all members of the shop every morning.


Only one project is taken by each senior cabinetmaker at a time, ensuring that each piece of furniture is guided carefully from the drawing phase through finishing stages and to the client’s home. We routinely receive compliments from our clients with respect to our professionalism and craftsmanship.


Our clients actively participate in the ongoing decisions affecting their projects and are encouraged to visit the shop during the construction phases. Most find this experience interesting and rewarding.

We at Bois De Vie are known for our contemporary, urbane and stylish interior design ideas. We work closely with our clients to come up with the proper ideas that suit their unique lifestyle. We then execute and deliver best in class interiors and exteriors for all types of residential and corporate spaces. We undertake and manage projects on a turn-key basis starting from idea to design to execution to maintenance.

Wood Species

Our experience in the trading of precious timbers and veneers is a big asset for our clients in choosing and developing their final product design


Finishing System’s Characteristics

BDVs finishing system is a deep penetrating finish and not a film forming finish. Advantages of a penetrating finish is the elasticity of the finish which is flexible enough to move with wood as it shrinks and swells


Wood Tones

We can benefit, our clients with the latest trends in: finishing paint products (Acid Curing Lacquers and Teflon based lacquers).


Technical Drawings & Mock-ups

In the development process of the project we give support to our clients in clarifications drawings, value engineering, and our extensive library of real wood and veneer samples help to support their design image.


Maintenance Methods

Wood and veneer are affected by light. For the first couple of weeks, do not place any items on the wood pieces as it is absorbing the UV rays which changes the color of the wood to a more homogeneous tonality.
